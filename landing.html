About us

Welcome to Cakes&Bakes, where every bite tells a story of tradition, passion, and love for baking.

At Cakes&Bakes, we believe that the best moments in life are shared over delicious, freshly baked treats. Our journey began in 2010, when our founder decided to turn their lifelong passion for baking into a cozy neighborhood bakery.

Since then, we have been dedicated to creating high-quality, artisanal baked goods that bring joy to our community.
